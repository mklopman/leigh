About me

Leigh is a New York-based writer and editor. Her primary passion is health and wellness, which manifests in the form of multiple yoga classes per week (shoutout to Sky Ting Yoga), long runs along the Hudson River, and the hundreds of articles that exist on the internet praising the mental and physical benefits of taking the best possible care of your body. Leigh's other passions include a holistic view of personal finance, 700-page novels, and feminism. She is forever on the hunt for trendy new garments with a strong feminist message, and she wrote a piece that went low-grade viral on why she split the price of her engagement ring with her fianc√©. You will never find Leigh without a book in her hand and a ripening avocado in her kitchen, because there's nothing she loves more than a good story paired with a slice of avocado toast with Trader Joe's everything (but the) bagel seasoning on top.


Leigh is currently Senior Editor at mindbodygreen, where she covers fitness, happiness, and relationships, and a regular contributor to Clarity Money (see where the personal finance piece comes in?). She previously worked at Elite Daily, where she built the health and wellness section from the ground up, The Huffington Post, and J-14 magazine. In 2016, she published her first book, A Rainbow Of Smoothie Bowls, inspired by her carefully curated, smoothie-heavy Instagram feed. You can buy a copy here. Not impressed yet? She's also a certified yoga instructor. Boom.
